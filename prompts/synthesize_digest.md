Write a highly readable weekly digest for a biomedical PI.

Required sections:

1. Title and date range
2. Executive summary, 3-5 bullets
3. High-signal updates
4. New papers
5. New or updated clinical trials
6. Regulatory / commercial / company updates
7. Conference / news / web signals
8. Watchlist by modality
9. Skeptical read: what is strong, weak, uncertain
10. Items worth reading fully
11. Search appendix: queries run and source counts

Use embedded Markdown links. Avoid hype. Label uncertain, early, commercial, and non-peer-reviewed claims clearly. Do not provide medical advice.
